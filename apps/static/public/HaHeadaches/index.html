Headache
Pain in any part of the head is usually called a headache. There are many causes of headaches ranging from minimal to very serious causes.

Headache can be a pain in any part of the head, including the face, skull, etc. Headaches may interfere with one's ability to work or do ones normal daily activities. Some people have headaches on a daily basis while others hardly ever get them at all. Though headaches can be painful and stressful, they are rarely due to any serious conditions. Headaches not caused by another disorder are usually classified as primary headaches. Headaches caused by another disorder are classified a secondary headaches. Primary headache disorders include migraine, tension type headaches, and cluster headaches. Secondary headaches may result from disorders of the brain, eyes, nose, teeth, sinuses, throat, jaws, ears, and or neck.
